Module 5 — Evidence Integration Procedure

Step 5: Integrate evidence into reasoning

In this phase, evidence must be integrated into clinical reasoning so that it supports, clarifies, and justifies claims. Evidence that is present but not explained—or poorly connected—can lead to misinterpretation in healthcare communication.

In academic writing, this means explaining how research supports your point rather than simply attaching a citation to a sentence.

Model Documentation Statement

Evidence must be integrated into reasoning so that it clearly supports the claim being made. Simply including a source is not sufficient; the writer must explain how the evidence relates to the claim and what it demonstrates. Without this explanation, evidence may appear disconnected, reducing clarity and weakening the credibility of the communication.
